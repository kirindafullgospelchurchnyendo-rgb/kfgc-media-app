About Us

Kirinda Full Gospel Church is a Christ-centered, Spirit-filled community dedicated to reaching souls, transforming lives, and raising a generation that walks in God’s purpose. We are a warm, welcoming family committed to worship, discipleship, and service.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ, make disciples, strengthen families, and build a loving community grounded in faith, holiness, and service.

Our Vision

To be a Church that transforms lives, communities, and nations through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Core Values

  • Faith – Trusting God in all things.
  • Love – Showing compassion to everyone.
  • Holiness – Living a life that honors God.
  • Service – Serving the community with joy.
  • Unity – Working together as one family.

Our History

Kirinda Full Gospel Church began in 2015 with a small group of believers meeting for prayer. Through God’s grace, the church has grown into a vibrant community dedicated to worship, teaching, and outreach—impacting lives in Kirinda, Nyendo, and beyond.
